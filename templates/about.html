About Dental OPG AI

An advanced AI diagnostic system for panoramic dental X-ray analysis and disease detection

System Overview

The Dental OPG AI Diagnostic System is an intelligent platform designed to assist dental professionals in analyzing panoramic dental X-ray (OPG) images. Using advanced deep learning technology, the system provides rapid, accurate classification of dental conditions with high confidence scores.

This system serves as a preliminary screening tool to help dentists identify potential dental abnormalities and conditions that might require further investigation or intervention.

Key Features

Fast Analysis

Real-time instant classification of OPG images within seconds

Confidence Scores

Data-backed visualization of prediction probabilities for all conditions

OOD Detection

Smart filtering prevents analysis of non-medical or invalid images

Secure & Private

User authentication and encrypted data storage for privacy protection

History Tracking

Complete scan history with predictions and results archiving

Report Generation

Professional PDF reports with findings and confidence metrics

Detectable Conditions

Caries

Early detection of tooth decay and cavity formation for timely intervention and prevention of progression

Decayed Tooth

Identification of severely decayed or damaged teeth requiring urgent dental treatment

Ectopic Tooth

Detection of abnormally positioned or impacted teeth requiring specialist evaluation

Healthy Teeth

Classification of normal, healthy tooth structures with no visible pathology
